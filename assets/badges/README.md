# Partner emblem badges

These are **placeholders**. Replace each with the official artwork (keep the same
filenames, or update the `src` in `index.html` / `about.html`). Transparent PNG or SVG
works best — the badge frame gives it a dark disc + gold ring, so a logo that reads on
dark is ideal.

| File | What to drop in | Where to get it |
|------|-----------------|-----------------|
| `microsoft.svg` (or .png) | Your official Microsoft partner badge | Partner Center → **Logo Builder** (partner.microsoft.com) |
| `cisco.svg` (or .png) | Your official Cisco partner logo | **cisco.com/go/partnerbranding** → Partnership Logo Builder (partner login) |
| `usmc-veteran.svg` (or .png) | The free **USMC Veteran insignia** | Marine Corps Trademark Licensing Office — **trademark.marines.mil** (veteran insignia is free for veteran-owned businesses) |

**Brand-guideline notes:**
- Don't recolor or distort the Microsoft/Cisco logos. The frame adds the gold ring *around* them, with padding for clear space — the logos themselves stay untouched.
- Use the USMC **Veteran insignia**, not the Eagle-Globe-Anchor — the EGA needs a paid commercial license; the veteran insignia is provided free for exactly this use.

After replacing a file, hard-refresh (Ctrl+F5) to see it.
